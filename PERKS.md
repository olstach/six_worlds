# Six Worlds - Perks: Space, Air, Fire

> **Stacking rule:** Percentage-based cost reductions (mana, stamina) stack **multiplicatively**, not additively.
> Example: -75% (school) + -25% (ritual circle) = 81.25% total reduction, not 100%.
>
> **Overcast stacking:** Spellpower multipliers from overcast effects (Push the Words, Burn the Breath, Grand Working, One Perfect Sentence) stack **additively** with each other.
> Example: Push (+100%) + Burn (+100%) = +200% total, not x2 x 2 = x4.

**Parry**
_Requires: Swords 3_
Passive. 20% of your Attack is added to Armor while wielding a sword. Does not apply if flanked.

**Don't Lose the Momentum**
_Requires: Swords 4_
Passive. While you successfully parried last turn, sword attacks cost 1 less Stamina and your Parry threshold is treated as 5% higher.

_The guard doesn't lower until it's decided to._

**Improved Parry**
_Requires: Swords 7, Parry_
Passive. 40% of your Attack is added to Armor. Parry applies against the first melee hit each round even if flanked.

**Measured Strike**
_Requires: Swords 1_
Active (3 Stamina). Make an attack with +20% accuracy. On hit, gain +15% Armor for 1 turn.

_The sword teaches restraint before aggression._

**Read the Line**
_Requires: Swords 1_
Passive. Sword attacks against enemies who moved toward you this turn gain +15% damage and +10% accuracy.

_The sword knows where the enemy will be._

**Opportunist**
_Requires: Swords 1_
Passive. While wielding a sword, enemies cannot move through your tile or any tile adjacent to you without provoking a free sword attack (once per enemy per turn).

_The line is not drawn on the ground. It passes through you._

**Lunge**
_Requires: Swords 2_
Active (4 Stamina). Leap up to 2 tiles toward an enemy and make a sword attack with +25% damage.

**Riposte**
_Requires: Swords 5_
Passive trigger. When you successfully Dodge or Parry a melee attack, your next sword attack costs 2 less Stamina and gains +25% damage.

_Defense is stored motion._

**Stand in the Gap**
_Requires: Swords 5_
Active. End your turn. Until your next turn, enemies moving past or attacking allies within 1 tile of you provoke a free sword attack.

**Found the Rhythm**
_Requires: Swords 6_
Passive. Against enemies you have already hit once this combat, sword accuracy is treated as 10% higher.

_Familiarity is already half the fight._

**Counterstrike**
_Requires: Swords 7_
Active (6 Stamina, 3-turn cooldown). Until your next turn, each melee attack against you triggers a counterattack (once per attacker per round).

**Surgical**
_Requires: Swords 8_
Passive. Sword attacks against targets below 40% HP deal +20% damage and cannot be parried.

_The clean ending is its own technique._

**Final Cut**
_Requires: Swords 10_
Active (8 Stamina). A precise strike that ignores 50% of Armor and 50% of Resistances. If it kills the target, refund 4 Stamina.

_Conflict ends when the line is drawn cleanly._

**Centered Stance**
_Requires: Swords 3_
Passive. +10% Initiative and +15% resistance to mental effects while wielding a sword.

_Stillness before motion._

**One Line, One Breath**
_Requires: Swords 7, Yoga 1_
Passive. The first sword attack each combat has +25% accuracy and cannot critically fail.

**Stone Covenant**

**Prepared Caster**

**Void Necromancy**
_Requires: Black Magic 5, Space Magic 3_

## Elemental Mastery 🌀

> These unlock based on total element affinity — the sum of all your skill levels in that element's 7 skills. Reaching **40 affinity** requires deep specialization across multiple skills in that element (e.g., four skills at 7+, or a broad spread reaching 40 total). These are late-game capstones.

**Void Adept**
_Requires: Space affinity 25+_
Space magic mana costs reduced by 15% (multiplicative). +10% to all Space-element skill checks. Once per rest, cast your next Space spell as a free action (no action cost).

_You have touched the emptiness between things._

**Wind Adept**
_Requires: Air affinity 25+_
+1 permanent Movement Speed. Air damage +15%. Immune to Slow effects.

_The wind does not ask permission._

**Flame Adept**
_Requires: Fire affinity 25+_
Fire damage +15%. Immune to fear and morale penalties. Leadership and Performance effect durations +1 turn.

_Passion is not consumed — it is shared._

**Tide Adept**
_Requires: Water affinity 25+_
All healing effects +15% (given and received). +20% resistance to water and ice damage. Can breathe underwater (event use).

**Stone Adept**
_Requires: Earth affinity 25+_
+10% max HP. +10% Armor. Crafted items are one quality tier higher. Immune to knockback and forced movement.
