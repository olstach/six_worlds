Spells come in five levels, starting with 1. Spells connected with two or more schools require only on of them at the requisite level, but bonuses from all appropriate skills apply. Some spells may require more than one skill, which should be openly stated. Skills may have tags, which may or may not be visible to the player and which are used to fine grain which spells are affected by specific upgrades and effects. Those tags may work similar to skills in HoMM, with three levels changing the damage, duration and aoe - e.g. buff and debuff spells may scale as follows: without upgrade, one target; first tier upgrade, AoE; second tier upgrade, all allies/enemies.
