# Hungry Ghost Realm Events — Draft Plan

## Realm Themes
- **Insatiable hunger and craving** — the defining trait of pretas
- **Decay, rot, and impermanence** — everything is falling apart
- **Attachment to the past** — ghosts clinging to former lives
- **Three zones**: Fetid Swamps (rolang/zombies), Dry Graveyards (skeletons), Charnel Grounds (vetalas)

## Karma Guidelines
- Feeding/sharing → god, human karma
- Greed/hoarding → hungry_ghost karma
- Violence against undead → hell, asura karma
- Compassion/release → god karma
- Trickery/theft → animal, hungry_ghost karma
- Buddhist practice (yoga, ritual) → god karma

## Batch 1: Events 1–20

### 1. hg_starving_preta — "The Starving Spirit"
A emaciated preta with a distended belly and needle-thin throat begs for food. Its voice is barely a whisper.
- **Give rations** (default) — costs food, god/human karma, XP
- **Medicine check** (requirement: medicine 2) — diagnose its condition, teach it to eat slowly, bonus XP + god karma
- **Ignore it** (default) — no cost, hungry_ghost karma
- **Attack the weakened creature** (default) — easy combat vs lost_preta, hell karma

### 2. hg_bone_merchant_event — "Bone Merchant"
A skeleton in a tattered merchant's robe sits behind a blanket of wares laid on dry earth. It clicks its jaw in greeting.
- **Browse wares** (default) → shop: hg_bone_merchant
- **Trade skill** (requirement: trade 2) — get a discount, text outcome
- **Rob the merchant** (roll: finesse DC 13) — success: get item, hungry_ghost karma / fail: combat vs skeleton_warrior
- **Comedy** (roll: comedy DC 12) — tell a skeleton joke, success: free item + human karma

### 3. hg_restless_grave — "The Restless Grave"
The earth shifts. A hand pushes through the soil, then another. Something is trying to claw its way out.
- **Help it out** (default) — it's a confused rolang; roll awareness DC 12 to calm it, or combat
- **Dig it up** (requirement: strength 13) — unearth a buried warrior with equipment, get item reward
- **Perform last rites** (requirement: yoga 2) — put the spirit to rest, god karma + XP
- **Leave it** (default) — text, minor animal karma

### 4. hg_swamp_crossing — "The Fetid Crossing"
A wide stretch of black, stinking water blocks the path. Shapes move beneath the surface. The only bridge is half-rotted.
- **Cross the bridge** (roll: finesse DC 11) — success: safe crossing / fail: fall in, take damage + combat vs swamp_vermin
- **Wade through** (requirement: constitution 14) — brute force, take minor damage but get through
- **Find another way** (requirement: awareness 13) — spot a hidden path, XP reward
- **Swim across** (default) — combat vs bog_zombie, guaranteed encounter

### 5. hg_wandering_monk — "The Dead Monk"
A skeleton in saffron robes sits in perfect lotus position on a flat stone. Prayer beads still wrapped around its bony fingers. As you approach, its jaw opens: "I have been waiting. Not for you specifically. Just... waiting."
- **Sit and meditate together** (requirement: yoga 3) — deep conversation about impermanence, large XP + god karma
- **Ask for teaching** (default) — skill training opportunity (white_magic or yoga), costs gold
- **Ask about the realm** (default) — lore text about the hungry ghost realm, small XP
- **Steal the prayer beads** (roll: finesse DC 14) — success: get prayer_beads / fail: the monk is disappointed (god karma loss)

### 6. hg_preta_pack — "Hungry Crowd"
A pack of pretas surrounds you, moaning and reaching with skeletal hands. They don't attack — they beg. Their eyes are desperate.
- **Share your supplies** (default) — costs rations, god/human karma, XP
- **Intimidate them away** (roll: strength DC 12) — success: they scatter / fail: combat vs rolang_mob
- **Persuade them to a water source** (requirement: persuasion 2) — lead them to relief, large god karma
- **Fight through** (default) — combat vs rolang_mob, hell/asura karma

### 7. hg_charnel_ground_entrance — "The Charnel Grounds"
Smoke rises from ritual fires. Human bones are arranged in geometric patterns on the ground. A vetala perches on a skull-pile, watching you with amused curiosity. "Another living one. How delightful."
- **Ask for passage** (default) — the vetala demands a toll (gold), text outcome
- **Challenge the vetala** (default) — combat vs vetala_scout, asura karma
- **Impress with magic** (requirement: black_magic 3) — the vetala is intrigued, grants passage + teaches a spell
- **Offer to trade stories** (requirement: charm 14) — entertain the vetala, XP + human karma, safe passage

### 8. hg_grave_robbers — "Grave Robbers"
Two skeletons are prying open a stone sarcophagus. They freeze when they see you.
- **Help them** (default) — split the loot, get item, hungry_ghost karma
- **Stop them** (default) — combat vs skeleton_warrior x2, god karma
- **Demand a cut** (requirement: charm 12) — intimidate them into sharing, get item, no karma shift
- **Report to the bone patrol** (roll: awareness DC 13) — success: find a skeleton patrol, get XP reward / fail: the robbers attack

### 9. hg_teahouse_event — "The Last Cup"
In an impossible pocket of warmth, a skeletal figure in fine robes serves tea to the dead. The smell is real — jasmine and smoke. A sign reads: ALL ARE WELCOME. PAYMENT EXPECTED.
- **Enter and rest** (default) → shop: hg_teahouse
- **Ask about the other patrons** (default) — lore text, learn about the realm's factions
- **Perform for the crowd** (roll: performance DC 12) — success: earn tips (gold) + human karma / fail: awkward silence
- **Offer to help serve** (requirement: grace 2) — work for your meal, free rest + human karma

### 10. hg_undead_caravan — "The Dead Caravan"
A line of skeletons carrying bundles marches along a dusty road. A skeleton with a feathered hat rides at the front on a skeletal horse. "We trade between the cities of the dead. Care to join us for a stretch?"
- **Travel with them** (default) — safe passage to next area, small XP
- **Trade with the caravan** (default) → shop: hg_bone_merchant (different inventory flavor text)
- **Offer guard services** (requirement: strength 13 or swords 2) — protect them from raiders, combat vs rolang_mob, good rewards
- **Ambush the caravan** (default) — combat vs skeleton_warband, large loot, hell + hungry_ghost karma

### 11. hg_wailing_well — "The Wailing Well"
A stone well stands alone in a dead field. Wailing rises from its depths — not wind, but voices. Something glints at the bottom.
- **Lower yourself down** (roll: finesse DC 13) — success: find treasure (gold + item) / fail: take fall damage
- **Listen to the voices** (requirement: awareness 14) — hear a prophecy/clue about the realm, large XP
- **Throw in an offering** (default) — costs gold, the wailing stops, god karma
- **Seal the well** (requirement: earth_magic 2) — permanently silence it, god + human karma, XP

### 12. hg_vetala_riddler — "The Riddler"
A vetala hangs upside-down from a dead tree, grinning. "Answer my riddle, traveler, and I'll let you pass with a gift. Fail, and I take something of yours."
- **Accept the challenge** (roll: awareness DC 14) — success: item reward + XP / fail: lose gold or item
- **Refuse and fight** (default) — combat vs vetala_scout
- **Counter with your own riddle** (requirement: learning 3) — impress the vetala, bonus reward
- **Trick the vetala** (roll: guile DC 15) — success: steal its treasure without answering / fail: combat vs vetala_pack

### 13. hg_burial_mound — "The Burial Mound"
An ancient mound rises from the swamp, its entrance marked with faded symbols. Cold air breathes from the opening.
- **Enter the mound** (default) — explore, find treasure but trigger combat vs rotting_sentinel
- **Read the symbols** (requirement: learning 2) — understand the warnings, enter safely, better loot
- **Perform a ritual** (requirement: ritual 2) — consecrate the mound, god karma + XP, no loot
- **Collapse the entrance** (requirement: earth_magic 1) — seal whatever's inside, small XP, safe

### 14. hg_corpse_flower — "The Corpse Flower"
An enormous flower blooms in the swamp, its petals the color of bruised flesh. Its scent is overpowering but strangely alluring. Dead insects litter the ground around it.
- **Harvest it** (requirement: alchemy 2) — get rare reagents, XP
- **Resist the scent and pass** (roll: constitution DC 12) — success: pass safely / fail: poisoned status + combat vs swamp_vermin (lured by your stumbling)
- **Study it** (requirement: medicine 2) — identify it as medicinal, get herb_bundle + XP
- **Burn it** (default) — destroy the hazard, earth karma, small XP

### 15. hg_skeleton_king_herald — "The Herald"
A golden skeleton in ornate armor blocks the road. Its voice booms: "The Skeleton King of the Setting Sun demands tribute from all who pass through his domain. Pay, serve, or be added to the collection."
- **Pay tribute** (default) — costs moderate gold, safe passage, text
- **Refuse and fight** (default) — combat vs skeleton_elite, asura karma
- **Claim diplomatic immunity** (requirement: persuasion 3) — bluff your way through, XP + human karma
- **Offer military service** (requirement: swords 3 or axes 3) — join a patrol mission, combat vs rolang_mob, paid in gold + XP

### 16. hg_desperate_mother — "The Mother"
A rolang woman clutches a bundle to her chest, rocking back and forth. The bundle is empty — just rags. She doesn't seem to know. "Have you seen my child? I just need to feed them. Just once more."
- **Comfort her gently** (default) — she fades away, god + human karma, XP
- **Use yoga to help her release** (requirement: yoga 3) — guide her to acceptance, large god karma + XP
- **Tell her the truth** (roll: charm DC 14) — success: she finds peace / fail: she attacks in grief (combat vs swamp_wraith)
- **Leave quietly** (default) — text, small animal karma

### 17. hg_mercenary_lodge — "Undertakers' Lodge"
A squat building of stacked bones, its door marked with crossed shovels. Inside, undead mercenaries sit sharpening weapons and arguing about pay rates. A notice board covers one wall.
- **Enter the lodge** (default) → shop: hg_mercenary_guild
- **Check the notice board** (default) — bounty hunting opportunities, text with lore
- **Challenge a mercenary to a spar** (roll: strength DC 13) — success: earn respect + XP / fail: lose gold
- **Offer your services** (requirement: might 2) — take a contract, combat mission, gold + XP reward

### 18. hg_poison_bog — "The Poison Bog"
Green mist clings to the surface of a bubbling swamp. The water hisses where it touches exposed bone.
- **Push through quickly** (roll: constitution DC 13) — success: get through with minor damage / fail: poisoned + more damage
- **Find a safe path** (requirement: awareness 14) — spot solid ground, safe passage + XP
- **Neutralize the poison** (requirement: alchemy 3) — clear a section, harvest reagents, XP + items
- **Use air magic to disperse the mist** (requirement: air_magic 2) — clear the path, XP

### 19. hg_town_event — "Crossbone Town"
A settlement of the dead — bone-walled buildings, skeleton merchants, ghostly lanterns. It's almost normal, if you squint. Guards at the gate eye you without hostility. "Weapons peace inside the walls. Break it and we break you."
- **Enter the weapons shop** (default) → shop: hg_town_weapons
- **Visit the magic shrine** (default) → shop: hg_town_magic
- **Buy supplies** (default) → shop: hg_town_supplies
- **Look for work** (default) — odd jobs, small gold + XP reward

### 20. hg_ancient_battlefield — "The Ancient Battlefield"
Weapons jut from the earth like iron flowers. The ground is more bone than dirt. Some of the fallen still twitch, trapped between death and something worse.
- **Scavenge for equipment** (default) — find a random weapon, risk combat vs bone_pack
- **Put the fallen to rest** (requirement: white_magic 2) — consecrate the field, large god karma + XP
- **Raise reinforcements** (requirement: black_magic 3) — gain a temporary undead ally for next combat, hungry_ghost karma
- **Train among the fallen** (requirement: martial_arts 2) — practice forms, gain combat XP
