# Hungry Ghost Realm Events — Draft Plan

## Realm Themes
- **Insatiable hunger and craving** — the defining trait of pretas
- **Decay, rot, and impermanence** — everything is falling apart
- **Attachment to the past** — ghosts clinging to former lives
- **Three zones**: Fetid Swamps (rolang/zombies), Dry Graveyards (skeletons), Charnel Grounds (vetalas)

## Karma Guidelines
- Feeding/sharing → god, human karma
- Greed/hoarding → hungry_ghost karma
- Violence against undead → hell, asura karma
- Compassion/release → god karma
- Trickery/theft → animal, hungry_ghost karma
- Buddhist practice (yoga, ritual) → god karma

## Batch 1: Events 1–20

### 1. hg_starving_preta — "The Starving Spirit"
A emaciated preta with a distended belly and needle-thin throat begs for food. Its voice is barely a whisper.
- **Give rations** (default) — costs food, god/human karma, XP
- **Medicine check** (requirement: medicine 2) — diagnose its condition, teach it to eat slowly, bonus XP + god karma
- **Ignore it** (default) — no cost, hungry_ghost karma
- **Attack the weakened creature** (default) — easy combat vs lost_preta, hell karma

### 2. hg_bone_merchant_event — "Bone Merchant"
A skeleton in a tattered merchant's robe sits behind a blanket of wares laid on dry earth. It clicks its jaw in greeting.
- **Browse wares** (default) → shop: hg_bone_merchant
- **Trade skill** (requirement: trade 2) — get a discount, text outcome
- **Rob the merchant** (roll: finesse DC 13) — success: get item, hungry_ghost karma / fail: combat vs skeleton_warrior
- **Comedy** (roll: comedy DC 12) — tell a skeleton joke, success: free item + human karma

### 3. hg_restless_grave — "The Restless Grave"
The earth shifts. A hand pushes through the soil, then another. Something is trying to claw its way out.
- **Help it out** (default) — it's a confused rolang; roll awareness DC 12 to calm it, or combat
- **Dig it up** (requirement: strength 13) — unearth a buried warrior with equipment, get item reward
- **Perform last rites** (requirement: yoga 2) — put the spirit to rest, god karma + XP
- **Leave it** (default) — text, minor animal karma

### 4. hg_swamp_crossing — "The Fetid Crossing"
A wide stretch of black, stinking water blocks the path. Shapes move beneath the surface. The only bridge is half-rotted.
- **Cross the bridge** (roll: finesse DC 11) — success: safe crossing / fail: fall in, take damage + combat vs swamp_vermin
- **Wade through** (requirement: constitution 14) — brute force, take minor damage but get through
- **Find another way** (requirement: awareness 13) — spot a hidden path, XP reward
- **Swim across** (default) — combat vs bog_zombie, guaranteed encounter

### 5. hg_wandering_monk — "The Dead Monk"
A skeleton in saffron robes sits in perfect lotus position on a flat stone. Prayer beads still wrapped around its bony fingers. As you approach, its jaw opens: "I have been waiting. Not for you specifically. Just... waiting."
- **Sit and meditate together** (requirement: yoga 3) — deep conversation about impermanence, large XP + god karma
- **Ask for teaching** (default) — skill training opportunity (white_magic or yoga), costs gold
- **Ask about the realm** (default) — lore text about the hungry ghost realm, small XP
- **Steal the prayer beads** (roll: finesse DC 14) — success: get prayer_beads / fail: the monk is disappointed (god karma loss)

### 6. hg_preta_pack — "Hungry Crowd"
A pack of pretas surrounds you, moaning and reaching with skeletal hands. They don't attack — they beg. Their eyes are desperate.
- **Share your supplies** (default) — costs rations, god/human karma, XP
- **Intimidate them away** (roll: strength DC 12) — success: they scatter / fail: combat vs rolang_mob
- **Persuade them to a water source** (requirement: persuasion 2) — lead them to relief, large god karma
- **Fight through** (default) — combat vs rolang_mob, hell/asura karma

### 7. hg_charnel_ground_entrance — "The Charnel Grounds"
Smoke rises from ritual fires. Human bones are arranged in geometric patterns on the ground. A vetala perches on a skull-pile, watching you with amused curiosity. "Another living one. How delightful."
- **Ask for passage** (default) — the vetala demands a toll (gold), text outcome
- **Challenge the vetala** (default) — combat vs vetala_scout, asura karma
- **Impress with magic** (requirement: black_magic 3) — the vetala is intrigued, grants passage + teaches a spell
- **Offer to trade stories** (requirement: charm 14) — entertain the vetala, XP + human karma, safe passage

### 8. hg_grave_robbers — "Grave Robbers"
Two skeletons are prying open a stone sarcophagus. They freeze when they see you.
- **Help them** (default) — split the loot, get item, hungry_ghost karma
- **Stop them** (default) — combat vs skeleton_warrior x2, god karma
- **Demand a cut** (requirement: charm 12) — intimidate them into sharing, get item, no karma shift
- **Report to the bone patrol** (roll: awareness DC 13) — success: find a skeleton patrol, get XP reward / fail: the robbers attack

### 9. hg_teahouse_event — "The Last Cup"
In an impossible pocket of warmth, a skeletal figure in fine robes serves tea to the dead. The smell is real — jasmine and smoke. A sign reads: ALL ARE WELCOME. PAYMENT EXPECTED.
- **Enter and rest** (default) → shop: hg_teahouse

### 10. hg_undead_caravan — "The Dead Caravan"
A line of skeletons carrying bundles marches along a dusty road. A skeleton with a feathered hat rides at the front on a skeletal horse. "We trade between the cities of the dead. Care to join us for a stretch?"

### 11. hg_wailing_well — "The Wailing Well"

## Batch 3: Events 41–60

### 41. hg_skeleton_king — "The Skeleton King of the Setting Sun"
A city of bone rises from the dust — towers of fused ribcages, walls of skulls, gates of pelvis and shoulder blade. On a throne of vertebrae sits the Skeleton King, crowned with a circlet of hammered copper. His eye sockets burn with cold intelligence. "All the dead pass through my kingdom eventually. What makes you think you're different?"
- **Kneel and request an audience** (default) — he's amused, grants you a task: clear a rolang infestation for him, combat vs rolang_mob, reward = safe passage through his domain + gold + XP
- **Challenge his authority** (requirement: strength 16 AND charm 14) — he respects boldness, offers a formal duel; combat vs skeleton_elite (boss difficulty), win = legendary weapon + massive XP + asura karma
- **Offer magical knowledge** (requirement: focus 16) — trade arcane secrets, he teaches you a rare spell + large XP
