[[six worlds - spell list]]


group/mass variants of spells should be removed; the ability to cast those will be added as a skill bonus available for the respective school of magic; the same should be done with potent/great variants.

perhaps also mastery levels could be added in, in a similar vein to HoMM3, so that every spell would have a couple levels of effects corresponding with the caster's level; this seems worse though, as a) it requires much more thought to be put into every spell and into balancing them, which means there will be fewer spells and less variety, and b) how will it work with the multi-school system? in the case of a fire/earth/sorcery spell, percentage or numerical modifier based bonuses can be added for additional spellpower and easily calculated, unlike a more rigid tiered system.
